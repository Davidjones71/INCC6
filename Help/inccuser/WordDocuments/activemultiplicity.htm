Active Multiplicity

To use this analysis method select Maintain | Calibration | Active Multiplicity.

There is a set of calibration parameters for each material type. For active multiplicity verification the only quantity determined at present is the neutron multiplication; verification 235U mass is not determined. The neutron multiplication can be determined from the measured triples and doubles rates and six nuclear data constants. You will probably not need to change these constants from their default values:

1st factorial moment of thermal neutron induced fission of 235U:      2.414

2nd factorial moment of thermal neutron induced fission of 235U:     4.638

3rd factorial moment of thermal neutron induced fission of 235U:      6.818

1st factorial moment of fast neutron induced fission of 235U:            2.637

2nd factorial moment of fast neutron induced fission of 235U:           5.623

3rd factorial moment of fast neutron induced fission of 235U:            9.476

The calibration parameters can be printed with the measurement (detector) parameters by selecting the "Print calibration parameters" button.
