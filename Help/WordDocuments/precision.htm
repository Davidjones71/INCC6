Precision

Precision measurements test the short term system stability by determining whether the observed scatter in a series of doubles measurements is statistically consistent with the expected scatter. The tested value is chi-square. The upper and lower acceptance limits are determined by the number of cycles and the confidence level (default: 99%). No corrections are done to the doubles rates for this test, but the checksum test and the accidentals/singles test are applied. The result is the measured chi-square value, the upper and lower limits, and a pass/fail message.
