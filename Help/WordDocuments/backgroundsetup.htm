Background Setup

You can inspect and change the background count rates under Maintain | Background Setup. The background rates are the passive singles, doubles, and triples rates with errors (room background), the active background singles rate and error (AmLi source background), and the passive and active background rates in scaler 1 and scaler 2 (the extra scalers on the PSR, MSR4, 2150 and AMSR shift-register units). Normally you do not need to use the Background Setup option. After a background measurement has completed, the background rates are automatically stored.
