Dual Energy Passive Multiplicity Assay

The dual energy technique is included in the INCC code for evaluation. It has not yet been experimentally verified. The dual energy technique allows the average (α,n) neutron energy to be different from the fission spectrum neutron energy. The ring ratio (the ratio of the singles count rate of the inner to outer ring of 3He tubes) is used to estimate the average (α,n) neutron energy.
